Travel to France.

Paris

  1. Eiffel Tower

    2. If you are still wondering what to see in France, immediately go to its capital, because there are so many sights of Paris that even a partial study of them will not fit in one trip. The symbol of Paris, as you might guess, has become the main attraction of France - the Eiffel Tower - one of the most visited and recognizable architectural objects in the world.

  2. Louvre

    3. A former fortress, once a palace, and now a museum - the Louvre in Paris has experienced all these transformations in its lifetime, becoming the most visited museum, where millions of people from all over the world come every year. The most distant past and present are intertwined here into a single whole in hundreds of thousands of exhibits, of which only 35 thousand we can see with our own eyes. The point is that there will not be enough exposition areas to display all these values, while many exhibits also require special storage conditions.

  3. Versailles palace

    4. Having wondered what to see in France, go without hesitation to one of the most respectable cities located 20 km from the capital. After all, there is a luxurious and relatively young palace and park complex, which once served as the residence of the French kings, and today it has also become a famous landmark of France. This is the Palace of Versailles - an outstanding masterpiece in the history of world architecture. The layout of the park of the Palace of Versailles also represents the highest achievement in park art in France.
